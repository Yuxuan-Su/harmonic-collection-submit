Taiwanese Tomato Beef Noodles

Pressure cooker version

台式番茄牛肉面

THIS DISH ISRECOMMENDED BY TERESA SUN

Tomato beef noodle flavored instant noodles are Teresa's favorite, and one day when I was videoing with her she was eating them again, so I had a sudden whim, let me help you to restore the real tomato beef noodle. So I asked my aunt in Taiwan about the way to make beef noodles. After trying it out, I realized that it was actually quite easy and I ended up giving the recipe to teresa as well, hoping that she can make it herself in London.

STEP 1: Choose a good piece of beef tendon, clean it and cut it into cubes.

STEP 2: Cook the beef cubes with green onion and ginger to remove the foam.

STEP 3: Add cooking oil to the pan, pour in the beef cubes and stir-fry with
the bean paste and soy sauce.

STEP 4: After about three minutes, add boiling water and spices (dried chili
peppers, star anise, and allspice).

STEP 5: Pour into pressure cooker and select MEET mode.

STEP 6: At the end of the pressure cooker, pour back into the saute pan and
add the peeled tomato pieces.

STEP 7: Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta and vegetables (I
cooked baby bok choy).

STEP 8: Place the noodles and greens in a bowl, add the cooked tomato beef
broth and finish with the cilantro.
